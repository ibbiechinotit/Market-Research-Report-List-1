<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Directional Drilling Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional System</li><li>Rotary Steerable System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Directional Drilling Service market includes two primary types: Conventional Systems and Rotary Steerable Systems. Conventional Systems utilize downhole tools and drill pipes with manual adjustments to change the drill's angle and direction, allowing operators to navigate complex formations. In contrast, Rotary Steerable Systems enable real-time steering through integrated technology, allowing for continuous rotation while adjusting the drill's trajectory. This enhances accuracy, reduces the need for directional corrections, and improves overall drilling efficiency, making both systems vital for diverse drilling applications.</p></p>
