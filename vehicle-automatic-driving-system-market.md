<p><p>The Vehicle Automatic Driving System market is categorized into levels ranging from L1 to L4, indicating varying degrees of automation. L1 refers to basic driver assistance features, such as adaptive cruise control. L2 includes partial automation, where the vehicle can control steering and acceleration, but the driver must remain engaged. L3 allows conditional automation, with the vehicle handling most driving tasks in specific scenarios. L4 represents high automation where the vehicle can operate independently in specific conditions but may require human intervention outside those conditions.</p></p>
