<p><p>The Spring Return Double Acting Pneumatic Actuator market comprises three primary types: Rack and Pinion, Scotch Yoke, and Linear actuators. Rack and Pinion actuators utilize a gear system for rotational motion, providing a compact form factor ideal for high-torque applications. Scotch Yoke actuators convert rotary motion into linear movement, typically offering higher efficiency and thrust. Linear actuators produce direct linear motion and are often preferred for applications requiring straightforward positioning, each type serving diverse operational needs across various industries.</p></p>
