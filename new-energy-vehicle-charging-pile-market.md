<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) charging pile market comprises two main types: AC charging piles and DC charging piles. AC charging piles provide alternating current, suitable for slower charging often used at home or fleet locations, allowing for overnight charging. DC charging piles, on the other hand, deliver direct current, enabling faster charging that’s ideal for commercial charging stations and public infrastructure, significantly reducing downtime for vehicles. Both types play crucial roles in supporting NEV adoption and enhancing charging convenience.</p></p>
