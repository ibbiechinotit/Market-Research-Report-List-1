<p><p>The Disc Tube Reverse Osmosis (DTRO) membrane market primarily features two types: DTRO and DTNF. DTRO membranes utilize a disc-shaped configuration that enhances filtration efficiency and allows for high flow rates, making them ideal for various industrial applications. In contrast, DTNF membranes are designed for applications requiring lower fouling rates and improved recovery, focusing on energy efficiency and reduced maintenance. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals, showcasing their versatility in filtration technology.</p></p>
