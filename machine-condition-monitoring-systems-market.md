<p><p>Machine Condition Monitoring Systems are categorized into three main types: Online Machine Monitoring, Portable Machine Monitoring, and Others. Online Machine Monitoring involves continuous real-time data collection from machinery, often utilizing fixed sensors to analyze performance and detect issues proactively. Portable Machine Monitoring, on the other hand, relies on handheld devices for periodic assessments, allowing flexibility and convenience for on-site evaluations. The "Others" category may include specialized or hybrid systems that combine features of both types or cater to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
