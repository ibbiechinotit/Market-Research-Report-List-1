<p><p>The rechargeable implantable pulse generator (IPG) market comprises unipolar and multipolar devices. Unipolar generators use a single electrode to deliver stimulation, providing a simpler design and often lower energy consumption, making them suitable for certain therapies. In contrast, multipolar generators utilize multiple electrodes, allowing for more complex stimulation patterns and greater precision, which can improve therapeutic outcomes. Both types cater to different patient needs and medical conditions, influencing their adoption in various clinical settings.</p></p>
