<p><p>The chlorinated polypropylene (CPP) market is primarily divided into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I CPP is characterized by its lower chlorination level and is commonly used for applications such as coatings, adhesives, and sealants due to its excellent flexibility and adhesion properties. In contrast, Type II CPP has a higher chlorination level, offering enhanced chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for demanding industrial applications. Each type serves distinct purposes based on its unique properties.</p></p>
