<p><ul><li>Epoxy Mineral Casting</li><li>Polymer Minreal Casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mineral castings market includes two primary types: epoxy mineral casting and polymer mineral casting. Epoxy mineral casting utilizes a resin-based binder, offering high strength, durability, and excellent thermal stability, making it ideal for precision applications in machinery and equipment. On the other hand, polymer mineral casting employs polymer-based binders, providing enhanced flexibility and resistance to environmental factors. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and machinery, catering to specific performance requirements and application needs.</p></p>
